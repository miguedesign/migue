CMG LOX

CUNA mutual group (CMG) - Loan Officers Experience (LOX)

  • Company frog

  • Business Type Financial Services, Insurance

  • Year Jan 2020 - Jun 2021

  • Role Interaction and Visual Designer

frog partnered with CUNA Mutual Group (CMG) to conceptualize, design, and develop a white-labeled digital platform for lending (auto, personal, and mortgage), account opening, and digital banking solutions to transform the consumer and Credit Union relationship through a modern user experience that enables trust and transparency.

The Project

frog designed and built the Digital Experience (DX) platform gradually included lending, mortgage and account opening MVP solutions for consumers and Credit Union staff. To support detailed design and development, frog built a design system as well as established a governance model for its maintenance and growth.

I was involved in the phase of expanding the BackOffice MVP to provide Loan Officers with tools to manage incoming Consumer Loans, thus creating an omni-channel experience for both members and the Credit Union employees that serve them.

Some screens of LOX

My Role

My role was hybrid, performing UX and UI tasks at the same time, on the one hand Interaction Design and on the other hand Detailed Visual Design.

Initially I was building the Back Office Experience (LOX) MVP, organizing the Information Architecture, creating, iterating and improving initial wireframes.

Improving my product design skills while collaborating with my peers, creating navigation patterns, evolving previously designed components and laying the foundation for the design system.

Tasks

Prototyping was one of the most continuous tasks, making low quality rapid prototypes, as well as high fidelity advanced prototypes to deliver to stakeholders or show the product vision to the engineering team that added to the documentation in the handoff.
