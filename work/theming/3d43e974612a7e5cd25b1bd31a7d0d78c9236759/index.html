DLS Theming

  • Company frog

  • Business Type Energy

  • Year 2022

  • Role Interaction and Visual Designer

frog worked with one of the Energy brands to define a clear path forward for the Energy Design System, while integrating other business verticals with similar use cases.

The Project

Phase 1

The first phase of the program focused on establishing ways of working between the design, technology and program management teams (Operation model). It also included the foundational work of the Design System, considering design principles and configuration for development.

The Project

Phase 2

The second phase applied what was established in the first phase, performing design and development sprints, where all teams implemented processes based on the operations model, adjusting and iterating these processes in each sprint. In this phase the design team focused on the UI Toolkit considering some components with an extensive and meticulous documentation with: states, variants, themes and accessibility.

DLS Multilevel

Organizing the project in figma was an important point in the project, to align design teams with different products and support various modes: Default, Dark, Dim, High Contrast and three density: Compact, Default, Relaxed.

Documentation

The documentation of the components for the whole UI ToolKit was approached on two fronts, on the one hand the documentation for the use by the designers and on the other hand the documentation of handoff and specs for its development by the engineering team.

The documentation included: Master component, Guidance & Usage, Handoff Specs, Samples, In Place.
