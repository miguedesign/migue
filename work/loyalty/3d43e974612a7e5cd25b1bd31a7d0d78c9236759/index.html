Loyalty

  • Company frog

  • Business Type Retail

  • Year 2021

  • Rol Interaction and Visual Designer

frog worked with one of the world's best-known fashion brands to redesign their digital loyalty experience. Together we created an engaging experience and laid the foundation for a new visual language that connects deeply with their audience.

The Project

We were requested to help a retail company with their new loyalty strategy and bringing it to their mobile app.

Collaborating with different areas focused on creating the business logic, we defined how this loyalty strategy architecture would be integrated to allow for scalability. Creating an account that would effortlessly deliver exclusive benefits through an attractive interface while maintaining simplicity and fun at all times.

Process and Rol

Once we understood the target audience, needs, desires and motivations for their connection to the brand. We explore various possibilities for the design of this new section within the application.

After an exhaustive analysis of the competition and incorporating insights, we had a period of feedback and collaboration with the client, which helped for the ideation and the direction that this new interface would take. We created a matrix that had as "X-axis" a range from +Conservative to -Conservative and in the "Y-axis" trigger questions with themes obtained in previous sessions with users.

Prototyping

In the process of exploring new ways to interact with the interface I was proposing, I made some prototypes that helped to understand the ideas I had.

In the testing phase, other prototypes that would be used by users were also made, so the detail was important to simulate a real scenario of the application. At the end of the testing experience we obtained favorable results with areas of improvement that were incorporated for a second phase of testing.

Documentation

All components and interfaces were documented for development by the engineering team. Starting with the smallest elements and ending with user flows and prototypes that would help in its development.

Detail design

Collaborating closely with other designers always helps to enrich the work. The fresh new look and feel of the redesigned loyalty experience was so well received by the client and users that, in the future, it was considered for incorporation into other digital channels.
